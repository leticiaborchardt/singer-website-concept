Abel Makkonen Tesfaye

Abel Tesfaye, known professionally as The Weeknd, was born on February 16, 1990, in Toronto, Canada. Raised in a multi-cultural environment, he was exposed to a diverse range of musical influences, from R&B and hip-hop to alternative and electronic music. Starting his career by anonymously uploading music to YouTube in 2010, The Weeknd quickly gained attention for his unique sound and compelling visuals.

Awards

The Weeknd has won several awards in 2023, including Grammy Awards and American Music Awards. He has also been recognized as one of the most popular and influential artists of the year.
